优先背 · 投智能客服

Story A · AI 玩家客服

左侧中文 · 右侧英文 90 秒稿 · 验收在 Cursor 说 验收英文

PM 词汇 · Story A（点开背）

resolution rate 解决率 · handoff 转人工 · threshold 阈值 · badcase 错例 · compliance 合规 · tradeoff 取舍 · rollout rollout · product owner · scope · baseline

S · 背景

海外游戏客户做 AI 玩家客服。上线前传统 Bot（无 AI、规则客服）只能兜住很少 FAQ；我来之前 AI 解决率仍约 3%，九成以上转人工，玩家也等得久。

S · Situation

We were building AI player support for overseas gaming clients. The legacy rule-based bot (no real AI) could only handle a small set of FAQs. When I joined, AI resolution rate was still only about 3%. More than 90% of chats went to human agents, and players waited too long.

T · 任务

我负责这条客服线：约 3 个月把自动解决拉起来，转人工可控，支付/到账不能乱答。

T · Task

I was the product owner for this line. In about three months, we needed a higher resolution rate, controlled handoff, and compliance on payment questions — no wrong promises.

A · 动作

  • FAQ 分域（支付库 / 玩法库），支付场景 threshold 更严
  • PRD：置信度低、敏感词、到账承诺 → 强制 handoff
  • 评测集 + badcase 回流，迭代 Prompt 和知识库
  • 和坐席同一套知识版本
  • 先打透 3 个核心市场，没有一上来铺 30 语言

A · Action

  • Split FAQs into payment and gameplay libraries; stricter handoff thresholds for payments
  • PRD: low confidence, sensitive words, or payout promises → forced handoff
  • Built an eval set and badcase loop for prompts and the knowledge base
  • Agents used the same knowledge version as the bot
  • Roll out to three core markets first, not 30 languages — a tradeoff on speed vs quality

R · 结果

约 3 个月：解决率 ~80%，转人工 ~97% → ~20%（同一批核心 FAQ、上线前后对比）。我是 product owner，拍清 scope、指标和跨团队节奏。

R · Result

In about three months, resolution rate reached ~80%, and handoff dropped from ~97% to ~20% (same core FAQ set, before/after). I drove scope, metrics, and cross-team delivery.

30 秒口播 · 中文

「我们做海外游戏 AI 客服。传统 Bot（无 AI）几乎兜不住 FAQ；我来之前 AI 解决率只有 3%，九成多转人工。我负责 3 个月把 AI 解决率拉到八成，转人工压到两成。支付玩法分库、阈值和支付强制转人工，badcase 闭环，和坐席同一套知识。」

90 sec · English（背诵版）

We built AI player support for overseas games. The legacy rule-based bot had almost no FAQ coverage. When I joined, AI resolution was only about three percent, and over ninety percent of chats went to agents. I owned this line for about three months. We needed higher resolution, controlled handoff, and no wrong promises on payments. I split FAQs into payment and gameplay libraries, with stricter handoff thresholds for payments. Low confidence, sensitive words, or payout promises meant forced handoff. We ran an eval set and badcase loop, and agents used the same knowledge as the bot. In about three months, resolution reached about eighty percent, and handoff dropped from about ninety-seven to twenty percent.

深挖转人工逻辑 → 置信度 / 阈值口播 + QA

今日主攻 · 坐席

Story B · 坐席 handoff / 自研工作台

S · 背景

客服 AI 解决率上来后转人工量仍大，会话走 外采第三方坐席，和自研 bot、知识库两套系统。坐席看不到玩家前面跟 bot 聊了什么，支付/合规上下文断，复盘培训都费劲。

T · 任务

推动 坐席工作台自研，和 AI 客服、外呼同平台；handoff 带 完整上下文 + AI 摘要，先保链路，再收效率指标。

A · 动作

  • MVP：实时会话、历史、监控、敏感词、支付队列
  • 自研周期长，按模块分批验收，优先 handoff 链路
  • 转人工屏必现：意向标签、合规 disclaimer、bot 过度承诺
  • 运营培训 SOP，和 Story A 知识版本一致

R · 结果

外采切到 自研工作台上线；handoff 上下文完整率明显提升（坐席少反复问玩家）。AHT 等效率数字在收，不硬编。

30 秒口播

「客服起来后转人工还是走外采，上下文断。我推自研坐席工作台，和 bot 同平台，handoff 带完整记录和 AI 摘要。先保支付队列和合规信息上屏，分批上线。自研替换外采后，上下文完整率明显好了。」

Story C · AI 外呼（拉新 / 拉回 / 活动通知）

仅营销触达 · 不答其他 · 不转人工

S：外呼做拉新、流失拉回、活动优惠通知，不是客服电话。

T：0→1 搭分类与活动脚本；只播预录脚本。

A：市场/分层脚本 · 过滤下线活动 · 不解答、不 handoff · 克隆改 1 分钟预录降本。

R：接通 30–50%；线路成本 ↓30–40%，获客单价 ↓20–30%。
